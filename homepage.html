Pokémon are fictional creatures that inhabit the Pokémon world, each with unique abilities and types, like Fire, Water, or Electric. Trainers, the humans in this world, capture Pokémon using Poké Balls and train them for battles. The goal for many Trainers is to become a Pokémon Master by defeating various opponents, including Gym Leaders and the Elite Four, while also completing the Pokédex, a device that records information about all Pokémon species. These creatures evolve, learn new skills, and form bonds with their Trainers, emphasizing teamwork and strategy in the Pokémon universe.
